About Beta Club

Beta Club is a club around the world that...

It's for everyone, expecialy those who'd like to become leaders. Between Divisions I and II of Junior Beta (grades 4-8) and Senior Beta (grades 9-12), the organization has helped nearly seven million students learn how to make a life for themselves—and,since 1991, it has awarded over $6 million in scholarships.

It has since done many things including fundraisers, going to the National Beta Club Convention, and more.

The Beta Club Pledge

As a Senior Beta,

I solemnly declare

That I shall always strive

To hold fast to the principals of honesty

To endeavor constantly to maintain a creditable record;

To cultivate in my life and conduct the principals

Of morality, service, and leadership.

I further pledge myself

To cooperate with the members of The National Beta Club

In the promotion of a sense of individual responsibility

To our school, community, state, and nation

To make right the master of might;

And to consecrate our comradeship to mutual helpfulness

And to the betterment of our fellow students.
